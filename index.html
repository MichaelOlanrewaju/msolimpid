About MSO Limpid

Oil & gas distributor at wholesale and retail level.

MSO Limpid Co. Ltd started operations in 2012 and was fully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in 2016. We are an authorised Mobil service station distributing petroleum products at both wholesale and retail level in Lagos.

We distribute PMS, AGO and LPG alongside genuine Mobil engine oils. We also operate a professional car wash and auto service bay.
