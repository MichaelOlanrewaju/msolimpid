Oil Change

Only genuine Mobil oil. Always.

Counterfeit engine oil is one of the biggest silent threats to Nigerian vehicles. At MSO Limpid, every oil change uses only genuine Mobil lubricants from the authorised Mobil dealer network.

Our technicians advise on the correct grade for your specific vehicle, engine type and driving conditions.
