Our Story

Built because Lagos deserves better.

MSO Limpid Co. Ltd started operations in 2012 and was fully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in 2016. We are into oil and gas distribution at wholesale and retail level operating as an authorised Mobil service station in Lagos.

“Our aim is to provide high-quality fuel products and services while prioritising customer satisfaction, environmental sustainability and community well-being.”
— MSO Limpid Co. Ltd

We distribute PMS, AGO and LPG alongside genuine Mobil engine oils and lubricants. We also operate a professional car wash and automotive service bay, making MSO Limpid a true one-stop facility for every vehicle need.

Our station operates 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Our Objectives

Seven commitments. Every day.

These specific commitments drive every decision made at MSO Limpid Co. Ltd.

Objective 01
Fuel Quality Assurance
Ensuring the quality and purity of PMS, AGO and LPG. Adhering to strict quality control standards and DPR regulatory requirements to guarantee the safety and performance of every fuel we supply.
Objective 02
Customer Satisfaction
Providing prompt fuelling services, maintaining clean and safe facilities, and offering convenient amenities including car wash and auto service. Every customer interaction is a priority.
Objective 03
Environmental Responsibility
Minimising our environmental impact through sustainable practices — reducing emissions, promoting energy efficiency and investing in eco-friendly technologies.
Objective 04
Safety & Compliance
Ensuring the safety of customers, employees and the community through strict safety protocols, regular maintenance inspections and full compliance with all health, safety and environmental regulations.
Objective 05
Lubricant Supply & Expertise
Providing expert advice on lubricant selection and application, ensuring availability of genuine Mobil lubricant products for all vehicle types and machinery.
Objective 06
Supply Chain Efficiency
Maintaining an efficient and reliable supply chain — optimising inventory management, minimising stock-outs and ensuring timely deliveries to meet customer demand.
Objective 07
Community Engagement
Being a responsible corporate citizen — supporting community initiatives, engaging in outreach programmes and contributing to local economic development through job creation.

The Founder

Idowu Egba.
The man behind MSO Limpid.

Idowu Egba founded MSO Limpid Co. Ltd in 2012 with a clear purpose — to operate a petroleum distribution business in Lagos that upholds genuine product quality, customer trust and community responsibility.

Under his leadership, MSO Limpid has grown from a retail fuel operation into a full-service facility offering wholesale and retail petroleum distribution, LPG supply, professional car wash and automotive maintenance.

He remains personally and operationally involved in the business — ensuring the standards he set in 2012 are maintained every day at every pump.
